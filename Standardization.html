Standardization¶

As I have learned recently from a recent model. Standardization is recommended before training any machine learning model. If you wanted to scale your data in unique methods, check out the documentation over in Scikit Learn's documentation. I usually just use the standard scaler in my model unless I notice something that makes me think different.
